What you need to know

To stay safe:

  • get vaccinated
  • wash hands regularly
  • open windows if meeting indoors
  • wear a face covering in crowded indoor settings
  • stay home if unwell

If you're worried

If your symptoms get worse or you need medical help:

  • use 111 online (opens in new tab)
  • in England or Wales, call 111
  • in Scotland, call a GP or call 111 out of hours
  • in Northern Ireland, call a GP or GP out-of-hours service
  • call 999 if you feel very unwell or think there's something seriously wrong

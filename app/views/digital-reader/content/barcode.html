What is the test strip ID number?

Check the expiry date on the box the test came in. Do not report the result if the test is out of date. Take a new test from a box that is still in date.
What to do with out-of-date tests

Do not use out-of-date tests. Recycle what you can and put the rest in your general rubbish bin.

The ID number is printed on the test strip under the QR code.

For example, LJU33456743
